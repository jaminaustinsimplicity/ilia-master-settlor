Start a New Kai-Zen Enrollment
Begin a new Kai-Zen IUL premium finance enrollment for yourself or an eligible member. The process takes approximately 20–30 minutes and your progress is saved automatically at each step.
info
Don't have a Kai-Zen policy yet? You're in the right place.
Kai-Zen is a premium-financed Indexed Universal Life (IUL) strategy designed to maximize your death benefit and retirement income potential. To be eligible, the insured must generally be between ages 18–70, in good health, and meet minimum income requirements. Your agent will confirm eligibility during the enrollment process.
How enrollment works
1
person
Personal & Insured Details
Provide the insured's name, date of birth, address, and contact information. If you are the insured, your details will be pre-filled.
2
health_and_safety
Health & Underwriting
Complete a preliminary health questionnaire. A full medical exam may be required by the carrier prior to policy issuance.
3
account_balance
Trust & Beneficiary Setup
NIW Trust Services will establish an irrevocable trust to own the policy. You'll designate trust beneficiaries at this step.
4
request_quote
Carrier & Product Selection
Your agent will present available carrier options and illustrations. You'll review projected values and select your preferred product.
5
draw
eApplication & Signatures
Complete the carrier eApplication and sign all required trust, loan, and enrollment documents via DocuSign.
6
check_circle
Policy Issued & Funded
Once approved, the carrier issues the policy, the trust is funded via the first tranche loan, and your policy appears in ILIA.
